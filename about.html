About Hotel Fort
Palace

"Where centuries of regal heritage whisper through the corridors of modern luxury."

Perched atop the ancient cliffs of the royal province, Hotel Fort Palace stands as a testament to architectural grandeur and historical resilience. Originally built as a defensive citadel for the reigning dynasty, the structure has been meticulously restored to preserve its majestic sandstone arches, intricate marble carvings, and panoramic vistas that once served as the watchful eyes of kings.

Our mission is to offer a sanctuary where the discerning traveler can inhabit history without sacrificing the zenith of modern comfort. Every stone here tells a story of valor, and every sunset viewed from our parapets connects the past to the present in a seamless golden hue.
