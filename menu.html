Malabar Lobster Curry

A tribute to the Malabar coast's maritime heritage. Succulent lobster tails are gently poached in a golden infusion of hand-pressed coconut milk, tempered with aromatic curry leaves and heirloom spices that have crossed oceans for centuries.

PROFILE: CREAMY - AROMATIC - HERITAGE

Guntur Lamb Fry

Experience the fiery soul of Andhra Pradesh. Prime cuts of tender lamb are slow-roasted with sun-dried Guntur chilies and a complex 'masala' ground fresh every morning. A bold, spirited dish that defines the rugged elegance of Deccan spice traditions.

PROFILE: ROBUST - PIQUANT - MASTERCRAFTED
